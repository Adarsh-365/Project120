About NamsteChina

Bridging the gap between Indian Buyers and Chinese Manufacturers with Trust & Transparency.

Who We Are

Namaste India Group is a Mumbai-based business facilitation group, actively working since 2021 to support Indian companies in expanding their businesses internationally. With a strong understanding of global trade challenges, we focus on creating trust, transparency, and seamless processes for Indian businesses entering international markets.


As part of this vision, we have launched our dedicated China trade initiative, Namaste China.


Namaste China is a specialized project designed to make importing from China simple, safe, and hassle-free. Our dual presence allows us to bridge the gap effectively.

The Problem We Identified

Many Indian companies want to import, but face significant hurdles.

Long Delays

Sample delivery often takes more than one month, slowing down business decisions.

Verification Issues

Difficulty in verifying whether the Chinese supplier is genuine or a middleman.

Quality Anxiety

Uncertainty whether the actual product quality matches online images.

Payment Risks

Concerns about payment safety and lack of recourse if things go wrong.

Our Solution

Namaste China provides an end-to-end import solution designed to eliminate risks. We sign MOUs with verified manufacturers and handle all on-ground operations.

  • Verified Chinese manufacturers (MOU signed).
  • Samples stored in our Mumbai Warehouse.
  • Indian buyers get samples in 7 days.
  • Full quality coordination & negotiation.

How It Works

01
